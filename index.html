What is an NFT?

A non-fungible token (NFT) is a unique unit of data stored online. NFTs can be used to represent easily-reproducible items such as photos, videos, and audio. These images are attached to a unique address online which gives them their individuality. In order to sell the rights to the specific NFT you must transfer the unique address that is associated with the picture, video and audio.
